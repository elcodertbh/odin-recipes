Tuna Melt

Description

Very simple but delicious tuna wrap recipe. Add mayo your tuna as desired, grill up some onions, add cheddar cheese, and wrap up in a spinach wrap. Mmmmmm.

Ingredients

  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Onions
  • Spinach wrap
  • Canned tuna
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Grill up some onions (half an onion)
  2. Mix tuna and mayo
  3. Place spinach wrap on griddle on low heat
  4. Add slice of cheddar cheese
  5. Place tuna and onions on cheese as it starts to melt
  6. Wrap up as desired
  7. Enjoy!
