Tamales

Description

Tamales are a steamed dough wrapped in a corn husk and stuffed with meat, usually pork or chicken. You get masa, the dough made of corn, spread it on the corn husk, add your meat, wrap it up, and place in a steam basket for approximately 1-2 hours (depending on the amount being made).

Ingredients

  • Masa
  • Stuffing (Meat or cheese)
  • Corn husks

Steps

  1. Make the filling
  2. Spread the dough on the corn husk
  3. Wrap it up
  4. Place in steamer
  5. Wait for 1 hour
  6. Enjoy!
